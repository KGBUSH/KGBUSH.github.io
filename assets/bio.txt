--- 2025.09.20
Yang DENG (Marco) is an ITF-funded Postdoctoral Fellow at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. His research focuses on "AI for sustainability," with a specific emphasis on smart energy systems. His work has been published in top computer science conferences and journals, including ACM Transactions on Sensor Networks, IJCAI, and NeurIPS, as well as top inter-disciplinary conferences such as ACM e-Energy and ACM BuildSys. He won the Best Paper and Best PhD Forum Presentation at ACM BuildSys 2024, and the Best Poster at ACM e-Energy 2024.
Marco received the B.Eng. and M.Eng. in Software Engineering from Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics and Tongji University, respectively. He received his Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computing at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University in 2024. Prior to his Ph.D., he worked for three years as a machine learning engineer at two of China's leading tech companies, Huawei and JD.com.
